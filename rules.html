Game Rules

Choose Your Category

Select a science category to test your knowledge. The available topics are Physics, Biology, and Chemistry.

Answer Questions

Attempt to answer as many questions as you can correctly. Your current score and the number of remaining questions will be displayed at the top of the page.

Scoring System

Each correct answer earns you 10 points. Strive for accuracy to maximize your score!

Game Completion

Once all questions are answered, your game statistics will be displayed. These include your total score, accuracy, and performance breakdown. You can choose to start a new game or check the Leaderboard, where the top 10 players are showcased.

Compete and Improve

Play again to challenge your previous scores and climb the leaderboard!

Good luck, and have fun playing the game!
